About

From the newsroom to the codebase.

I'm an incoming second-year Computer Science student at UBC, headed toward a co-op and a degree I plan to finish in four years — not because that's expected, but because I like having a plan and beating it.

My instincts are design and communication first: years of running PR for a youth org, heading a school newspaper's opinion desk, and producing a zine taught me how to make something land with the people it's for. Code and strategy are the parts I'm actively building — the gap between where I am and where I'm headed, and I'd rather close it in public than pretend it isn't there.

UBC is a launchpad, not the destination. The long game is running things — eventually, a company.

"I'm not the finished strategist yet. I'm the one showing the work in progress."
